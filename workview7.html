Hustle and Bustle 衣食住行
Silkscreen Print, Illustration
Odds and Ends Yale Art Book Fair 2018

Hustle and Bustle is a small book set of artist book using screen printing. It is a cooperative project, the right part is illustrated by Weiyun Chen, who is a graphic designer from China Taiwan, the left part is drawn by me from China Mainland. We share the similarity and differences between culture and customs through this book. Like two sides of a coin, the book has two sides which combine the different habit of flipping books in two places, from reading this book people can see what are the little differences between both sides of the Taiwan Straits in the theme of Wearing, Food, Resident and Transportation. This book was printed in CFA silkscreen studio with limited editions of 10 sets.
