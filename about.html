  • Xiqiao Chen is a graphic designer based in the Boston area, with a focus on publication design, identity systems, exhibition visual systems, and UI/UX design. Her practice is grounded in careful observation and driven by curiosity—constantly seeking new visual possibilities and conceptual clarity.

  • With an MFA from Boston University and a BFA from Sichuan Fine Arts Institute, her design approach bridges cultural narratives, blending Eastern and Western perspectives into cohesive, idea-led visual systems. She draws on both structure and emotion to craft thoughtful, context-sensitive solutions that resonate across disciplines and audiences.

  • Previously, she worked as a graphic designer at Lingjing Yang Studio in Beijing, collaborating with institutions such as the Goethe-Institut, 798 Art Bridge Gallery, Shanghai Translation Publishing House, Today Art Museum, Beijing Times Art Museum, and Chongqing Yunma Brand Design Consultant Co.

  • Whether designing for print, space, or screen, Xiqiao aims to create design that reflects, provokes, and connects—inviting viewers to question assumptions and discover new meaning through visual language.
