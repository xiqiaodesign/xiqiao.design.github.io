Where the wind Blows
Typography, Editorial
Vancouver Art Book Fair 2018

Where the Wind Blows, published by Open Rehearsal, features four sets of graphic explorations forhonor tiles sets. Each set played on the use of color, icons, Chinese characters, and phonetics toexplore the inherent meaning and function of each tile in the game of mahjong.

Published by Open Rehearsal, 2018
With contributions from Lily Chen, Winnie Chen, Mary Yang, and Rosa Yang
