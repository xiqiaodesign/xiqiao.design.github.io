Fifty lterations
Poster, Motion
2019

For this school project, we were assigned to find a masterpiece poster. We borrow the element from the original poster, using different forms and compositions to create our own piece. After finishing 50 posters, we designed a final piece which is inspired by the poster.

The poster I chose is Dick Elffers’ Holland Festival poster designed in 1965. The final output is an AR application combined with 50 name cards. Each card has a solid background, people can write their names or share contact information during the art festival. The AR application brings more interactive ways of entertainment.
